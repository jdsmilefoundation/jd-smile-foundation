About Us

Hamara sapna, hasi phailana. JD Smile Foundation ka mool uddeshya hai bachchon, mahilaon aur samaj ke prantik logon ke chehre par muskurahat wapas lana. Shiksha, swasthya aur manavik sahayata ke madhyam se hum ek samvedansheel samaj banane mein vishwas rakhte hain.

Our Work

  • Shiksha Karyakram (Education Programs)
  • Swasthya Shivir (Free Health Camps)
  • Smile Drive: Bhojan aur Kapde Vitran
  • Mahila Sashaktikaran aur Kaushal Vikas

Get Involved

Aap volunteer ban sakte hain, dan de sakte hain ya apne shahar mein fundraising karyakram chala sakte hain. Har ek sahayata se ek aur muskurahat banti hai!

Donate
