About Us

JD Smile Foundation aims to spread smiles and hope among underprivileged children, women, and marginalized communities. We focus on education, health, food drives, and environmental protection. In the future, we plan to open skill development centers, organize regular medical camps, and launch large-scale awareness campaigns to protect nature and empower communities.

Our Work

  • Education and Skill Development
  • Free Health Check-ups and Camps
  • Food and Clothing Drives
  • Women Empowerment Workshops
  • Environmental Protection Initiatives
  • Community Awareness Programs
