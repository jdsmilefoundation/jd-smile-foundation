About JD Smile Foundation

JD Smile Foundation is a community-driven NGO dedicated to spreading smiles through education, health support, and social empowerment. We work tirelessly to bring smiles back on the faces of underprivileged children, women, and marginalised communities. Our mission is to build a compassionate society where everyone lives with dignity and joy.

Our Work

  • Providing free education and school supplies to needy children.
  • Organising free health camps and awareness drives.
  • Food distribution drives for homeless and slum areas.
  • Women empowerment through skill training and self-help groups.
  • Environmental campaigns: tree plantation and clean-up drives.

Our Future Plans

In the coming years, JD Smile Foundation aims to:

  • Establish free evening schools in remote villages.
  • Build a community kitchen to feed 500+ people daily.
  • Launch skill-development centers for unemployed youth.
  • Expand environmental projects to plant 10,000+ trees.
  • Collaborate with other NGOs for larger impact nationwide.
